# Contributing

Thanks for looking. To be upfront about what this repo is:

**bending-spoons-ontology is a point-in-time demonstrative exercise by Play New** — a worked
example of how we model an organization as a queryable ontology + intelligence, built only
from Bending Spoons' public filings as of their dates (see the [README](README.md) and
[NOTICE](NOTICE)). It is **not actively maintained** and does not track the company over time.

Because of that:

- **Issues are welcome** — for discussion, questions, or to point out an error in how a fact
  is modeled or cited. That feedback is genuinely useful.
- **Pull requests are not being accepted.** There is no contribution pipeline and no CLA; the
  repo is a fixed snapshot, not a living project. The MIT license lets you **fork and adapt
  freely** — that is the intended path if you want to build on it.

If you fork it to model a different subject: keep the engine and the `foundations/` contracts,
swap the corpus in `sources/`, and keep every fact grounded in an official source — that is the
whole discipline.
