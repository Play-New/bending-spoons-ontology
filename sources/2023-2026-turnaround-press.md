# Capture — turnaround / restructuring press

Coverage of what Bending Spoons does after it buys, the restructuring lever. Verified examples:

- **Evernote** (acquired Jan 2023): laid off 129 staff in Feb 2023, then cut the remaining team and relocated operations to Europe in July 2023; free tier later restricted to 50 notes / 1 notebook (Dec 2023).
  - https://techcrunch.com/2023/02/27/bending-spoons-lays-off-129-evernote-staffers/
  - https://www.engadget.com/evernote-is-relocating-to-europe-after-laying-off-most-of-its-us-workforce-205012133.html
- **Tractive** (acquired May 2026, €770M): ~50% of jobs cut before the IPO.
  - https://www.trendingtopics.eu/bending-spoons-pays-e770m-for-tractive-then-cuts-50-of-jobs-before-ipo/

Note: larger cuts at WeTransfer (~75%) and Brightcove (reported 33% then higher) circulate in press/analysis but are not independently confirmed here; treat those exact percentages as `[to-validate]`. The F-1 confirms the *pattern* through recurring "separation package" cost lines across Issuu, Meetup, StreamYard, WeTransfer, Brightcove, komoot (`bsp-f1`).
