# Capture — Bending Spoons IPO pricing, 1 July 2026

The figures the F-1 left blank, from the IPO pricing (press, not the filing).

- Priced at **$29/share**, above the $26-28 range; raised **~$1.68B** (57,971,015 ordinary shares: 34,398,640 primary from the company + 23,572,375 from selling shareholders). Implied valuation **~$18.4B**, up from ~$11B in a private round eight months earlier.
- First traded on the **Nasdaq Global Select Market as "BSP" on 1 July 2026**; opened ~$31 and traded up sharply on the day (reported ~+42%). Bending Spoons rang the opening bell.
- **Q1 2026 (in the IPO coverage):** revenue $601M, more than double the $259M of Q1 2025; swung from a $112M net loss to a $27.5M net profit.
- CEO Luca Ferrari's stake was valued at ~$2.4B after the IPO. Company founded in Milan in 2013 by Ferrari and four co-founders after their first startup failed, leaving them ~$40,000.
- Joint lead book-runners: Goldman Sachs International, J.P. Morgan, Allen & Company.
