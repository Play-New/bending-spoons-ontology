## What it is
Tractive delivers solutions for monitoring the location and health of pets (bsp-f1 ~L397, bsp-424b4 ~L377). tractive GmbH is an Austria-based technology company, founded in 2012, that develops collar-mounted devices and a companion mobile application enabling pet owners to monitor their animals' real-time location, activity levels, and health metrics, supported by a subscription-based model (bsp-f1 ~L4597–4598, bsp-424b4 ~L4700–4701). It is one of Bending Spoons' few hardware products: the filings note Tractive depends on hardware devices and related accessories that require sourcing, manufacturing, warehousing, logistics, and distribution operations different from those of the group's purely software products (bsp-f1 ~L917, bsp-424b4 ~L898).
